Our Story

Welcome to Evergreen Bistro

Founded in 2010, Evergreen Bistro has been serving the community with delicious, sustainably-sourced meals that celebrate the bounty of our local farms and the changing seasons.

Our mission is simple: to provide an exceptional dining experience that connects our guests with the land and the people who cultivate it, while minimizing our environmental impact.

Local Ingredients

We source 90% of our ingredients from within 50 miles.

Sustainable Practices

Zero food waste policy and compostable packaging.

Seasonal Menu

Our menu changes weekly based on what's fresh and available.

Award Winning
