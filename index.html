Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for all party sizes. You can book a table through our website, by phone, or in person.

Is there parking available?

We have complimentary valet parking for our guests, as well as limited street parking in the area.

What is your dress code?

We recommend smart casual attire. While we don't have strict dress requirements, we ask guests to avoid beachwear and athletic clothing.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, our menu includes vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary needs.

What is your cancellation policy?

For reservations, we request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For private events, please refer to your contract for cancellation terms.
